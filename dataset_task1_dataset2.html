Question: How To Develop Good Study Habits for College

Target Answer:

To develop good study habits for college, find a quiet, dedicated space and create a consistent study schedule for yourself. Make sure you have everything you need to study at your space and eliminate all distractions, like your smartphone, while you're reviewing your materials. Figure out what topics are most pressing before each study session and try to tackle the hardest stuff first to make the most efficient use of your time. For tips on forming an effective study group with your classmates, read on!
