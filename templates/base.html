WikiHowQA

WikiHowQA is a pioneering dataset set to redefine the landscape of Multi-Document Non-Factoid Question Answering (MD-NFQA). Drawing from the rich "how-to" content of the popular WikiHow website, WikiHowQA shines a spotlight on this underrepresented category in current MD-NFQA datasets.
